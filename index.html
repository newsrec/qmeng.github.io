Resume

Meng Qing (孟青)

Qing Meng is a lecturer at College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Hohai University (HHU), professional member of CCF. He obtains his Ph.D. Degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University. His research interest is Social Media Analysis, Recommender Systems, Service Computing, NLPand CV.He is currently the principal investigator of four projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation, and enterprise-funded research projects. He has published more than 20 papers in prestigious CCF-recommended journals and conferences such as ACM TOIS, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TKDE, 计算机学报, and WWWJ. He has also applied for 5 national invention patents, of which 3 have been granted.

欢迎有意攻读计算机科学与技术、软件工程等专业的硕士研究生、并对社交媒体分析、推荐系统、服务计算、图神经网络、自然语言处理、计算机视觉等研究课题感兴趣的同学联系我。
